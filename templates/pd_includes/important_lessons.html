Important lessons

  • EC's can be a motivator, but also a constraint.

    One often does what they need to do and nothing more.

    EC's can be a great motivation, since you are forced to do the thing. But because of you being forced, it is much more diffucult to own what you make.

  • Knowing people can only help you.

    Sometimes it might feel difficult(and even scary) to get to know people, but the more people you network with the better. In the worst case, when a person does not like you, they will most often just ignore you.

  • It is okay to ask for help.

    This one is important!!! It is the same as with meeting new people. If you need help, you need help, and the worst thing the other person can do is to refuse.
