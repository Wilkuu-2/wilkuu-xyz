Self discovery

What profdev uncovered about me?

Well, despite of the low amount of time you actually spend on ProfDev, you learn a lot about yourself. This is what i learned:

  • Random bullshit go!!

    I discovered that I am really good on making things up as I go. Despite always wanting some plan set up, I quickly pivot away from the original plan.

  • Information Sponge

    Despite my belief that I'm no smarter than pile of rocks. My memory can hold a lot. It is not fully reliable but I remember a lot, quickly.

  • Sharp focus

    When I am able to focus on anything(which can be hard), there is a slim chance I stop focussing on it until it's done or I can't work on it anymore.
