My own custom note-taking system

As a student, I take a lot of notes on the computer. But just writing down what the teacher says is not sufficient in most cases. For this I created a note taking system.

It is designed based on how I remeber information the best, namely through:

  • Structuring it into sections and a indication on what type of information it is.
  • Typing the information

It follows a quite clean nested list structure design, that makes searching through it easier.

In addition to being really readable (at least for me), the process of writing in sections makes it easier to remember the content from just writing it down.

This system is not designed for universal use, rather being focussed on Study Material, Concepts and Structures, for things like journalling a more classic system is more useful

I started to design it at the beginning of September.
