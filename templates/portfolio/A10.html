Candidate sculpture for GogBot 2022

In module 4 of CreaTe we had a really free project topic. Our year is making candidate installations for the GogBot festival. What we came up with is a sculpture of a imposter Robocop-like entity. The entity has a screen where a questionaire is taken by the participant. This questionaire is designed to result in a social score, a parody of the Chinese social credit system. We are not yet done with this project

The demo-day is in the first week of july.
